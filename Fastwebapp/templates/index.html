F.A.S.T : Financial Stock Analysis- News Sentiment Analysis, Time Series Forecasting, Text Summarization of Financial meetings

We build our F.A.S.T product on basis of various research paper which have shown strong correlation between financial news and stock price reaction. The local media coverage and the financial news strongly predicts local trading, after controlling for earnings, investor, and newspaper characteristics.

In the United States, recent volatility levels rival or surpass those last seen in October 1987 and December 2008 and, before that, in late 1929 and the early 1930s. Motivated by these observations, we consider the role of the financial news on the stock market and how much it can impact the stock market to predict the trends in the stock prices.

The stock market has fluctuated heavily in recent times. We aim to find how much impact does the financial news have on the stock market trends.

What can F.A.S.T help you achieve?

To find the real time impact of Tweets, Financial news, Google Trends on the Stock Market

  1. Leveraging a real time AWS Architecture of data pipeline to result in web application & website which would let the user understand behaviour of the current and future of stock market
  2. Leveraging secured access via User Authentication Architecture for secured Access to F.A.S.T
  3. Applying sentiment analysis on Real time financial news & Real time Tweets to check its impact on the stock market
  4. Performing time series analysis which would forecast the stock prices of next months
  5. Performing Data Masking Operations which would protect user's personal information in financial meetings
  6. Influencing Company's Management Decisions Performing Financial Transcribed Actions & Text summarization which would give sentiment of Financial Meetings
